Deep in the mountains of Fujian, Master Li continues a 500-year-old tradition. Each bamboo strip is shaved to less than 0.5mm thickness.

![Master Li shaving bamboo](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1584622650111-993a426fbf0a?q=80&w=1000&auto=format&fit=crop)

The process requires extreme patience. One basket can take up to 2 weeks to complete.

"It's not just a basket, it's a piece of our history," says Li.
