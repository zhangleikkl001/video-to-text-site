The 28th Shanghai International Film Festival brings the “City Film Set” into real-life settings. Starting from May 20, two special exhibitions will officially open, and the festival’s themed blocks will gradually go live. **Changning District’s Xinhua Road Xingfuli Block** will be the first to transform into a Shanghai International Film Festival (SIFF) themed block.

**"SIFF City Cinema" Mini Program – Your Personal On-the-Go Guide**

Xinhua Road, Panyu Road, Xingfu Road... This neighborhood, naturally bathed in a cinematic glow, has long been a key "living room" for the Shanghai International Film Festival. This year, the festival has launched a brand-new **"SIFF City Cinema" mini program**, offering an interactive discovery experience that beautifully blends urban reality with the magic of film dreams.

![](/images/article/article-1779449369-cb4323/image_5.jpg)

Put on the headphones, and it becomes your personal audio guide. Strolling through the dappled shadows of plane trees, follow the neighborhood map at a leisurely pace. As you approach a spot, whispers of SIFF’s "time murmurs" will sound in your ears.

The streets and alleys beneath your feet may once have borne the footprints of cinematic masters; a building where you pause might have been the filming location for a classic movie. Join this urban wander, and amid the everyday hustle and bustle, piece together the scattered clues of light and shadow.

Swipe left or right to view images.**"Stars and Everyday Life" Special Exhibition – Reserve Your Visit**

Today, the Shanghai International Film and Television Festival debuts its first-ever special exhibition, "Stars and Everyday Life," now open to the public at Shanghai Film Art Center.

With a four-act narrative and over 500 rare items on display for the first time, multiple artists have come together to create an immersive experience. Film reels, a recreated red carpet, collectible ticket stubs—take a leisurely journey through cinematic memories. This is a public-interest exhibition, free of charge but with **advance reservation required**.
