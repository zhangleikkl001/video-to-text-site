Shanghai Urban Construction Vocational College (SUCC) is a public higher vocational institution under the Shanghai Municipal Education Commission. It is recognized as a national-level quality vocational college and a top-tier vocational college in Shanghai, with a 68-year history of education. The college focuses on urban construction, management, and services, offering a multi-disciplinary, applied technology-oriented education. Guided by the motto "Cultivate Virtue, Cultivate Talent, Cultivate Career," it emphasizes humanities, practice, specialization, skills, and internationalization, serving Shanghai's "Five Centers" and "Four Brands" strategies as well as urbanization nationwide. The college has six campuses in Fengxian, Yangpu, Baoshan, Jing'an, Wujing, and Guoke Road, covering over 800 mu, with more than 10,000 full-time students. It also oversees a national model secondary vocational school, Shanghai Construction Engineering School.

The college offers 45 majors, including Architectural Engineering Technology, Municipal Engineering Technology, Engineering Cost, Prefabricated Construction Engineering Technology, Ancient Architecture Engineering Technology, Cultural Relics Restoration and Protection, Modern Property Management, Big Data and Accounting, Smart Health Elderly Care Services and Management, Food Quality and Safety, Big Data Technology, Intelligent Control Technology, and Hotel Management and Digital Operations. Among these, 14 are national backbone majors and 8 are Shanghai first-class majors.

In accordance with the "Shanghai Municipality Public Institution Open Recruitment Measures" (Hurenshegui [2019] No. 15), adhering to the principles of openness, equality, competition, and merit, we are now openly recruiting high-level talents in disciplines, highly skilled talents from industries, excellent mature teachers, and outstanding fresh graduates.

I. Basic Requirements
1. Comply with the Constitution and laws of the People's Republic of China;
2. Passionate about education, with good political, ideological, and moral qualities, strong service awareness and dedication;
3. Possess the qualifications and abilities required for the applied position;
4. Physically and mentally healthy, able to perform duties normally and adapt to job requirements;
5. Applicants for permanent positions who are not Shanghai residents must hold a valid Shanghai residence permit for one year or more, with the calculation deadline being December 31, 2026. Those with senior professional titles are exempt from the one-year residence permit requirement.

II. Positions and Specific Conditions
15 professional technical positions. (See Appendix 1: Shanghai Urban Construction Vocational College 2026 Open Recruitment Plan for details.)

III. Recruitment Methods
1. Application Period
For teaching positions, the validity period is until October 31, 2026; for full-time counselors, until June 30, 2026. Applicants can select one position per recruitment stage based on their qualifications and interests. The number of positions in each department will be updated based on actual recruitment, and the process will end once filled.

2. Application Method
Applications should be submitted via email. Send the "Shanghai Urban Construction Vocational College Application Form" (see Appendix 2) and scanned copies of required documents to the HR email: zhaopin@succ.edu.cn, with the subject line format: "Applied College/Department + Position + Name + Graduating University + Highest Degree," e.g., "School of Municipal and Ecological Engineering + Municipal Ecology Specialty + Name + Tongji University + Ph.D."

Important: Applicants must follow the naming format strictly, avoid duplicate submissions, fill in the application form truthfully, sign and date the commitment section, and provide scanned copies of the application form, resume, highest degree certificate and online verification report, front and back of ID card, front and back of residence permit (for non-Shanghai residents), teaching and research achievements, certificates, etc. If the degree certificate is not yet available, provide the online verification report from Xuexin.com with QR code.

3. Qualification Review
The college will review application materials. Unsuccessful candidates will not be notified separately.
The qualification review runs through the entire recruitment process. If any applicant is found ineligible at any stage, their recruitment process will be terminated. Ensure your qualifications meet the requirements before applying.

4. Exams and Interviews
For teaching positions, each secondary college will organize trial lectures and interviews.
For full-time counselor positions, a written exam is organized by a third party, focusing on basic theories and professional knowledge. The passing line is the average score of the exam. Interview candidates are selected at a ratio of 1:3 based on written exam scores. Those not selected for interviews will not be notified. Interviews assess professional knowledge, abilities, and overall quality. The comprehensive score is 40% written exam and 60% interview.

5. School Approval
The Party and Government Joint Meeting of the secondary college (or department) reviews and submits the preliminary list of candidates for school approval, finalizing the list of proposed hires.

6. Background Check and Medical Exam
Medical exams follow the "National Civil Service General Medical Standards (Trial)" and must be conducted at a Grade 2A or above hospital locally. The HR department and employing department conduct background checks on candidates' political integrity, law-abiding record, moral character, and credit history.

7. Determination and Publicity of Proposed Hires
Based on assessment, background check, and medical exam results, the list of proposed hires will be published on the Shanghai Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau's official website for 7 days. If there are no objections, successful candidates for permanent positions will be submitted to the Shanghai Municipal Education Commission and the Shanghai Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau for approval. If there are objections affecting employment, the decision will be made based on investigation results.

IV. Related Benefits and Other Matters
Once hired, salaries, benefits, social insurance, etc., will follow Shanghai municipal and college regulations. Returnees from overseas must provide degree certification from the Chinese Service Center for Scholarly Exchange. If domestic fresh graduates fail to graduate on time, the employment offer is void.
