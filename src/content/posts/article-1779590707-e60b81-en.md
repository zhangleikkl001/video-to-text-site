**Kanas — The Back Garden Forgotten by the Gods in Northern Xinjiang**

![](/images/article/article-1779590707-e60b81/image_4.jpg)

Someone once said:

If you miss the autumn in Kanas,

you miss the entire autumn.

![](/images/article/article-1779590707-e60b81/image_5.jpg)

The autumn of Kanas,

Is like a palette of spilled paints,

Splashed across the color board,

Spreading a riot of purple and red.

Only by arriving in Kanas can one truly grasp

The meaning of Su Shi’s verse:

“Remember, the finest scenery of the year,

Is when oranges turn golden and tangerines green.”

**Greater Khingan Range — The Green Gem on China's Golden Rooster**

![](/images/article/article-1779590707-e60b81/image_9.jpg)

Looking down at the Greater Khingan Range,

it resembles a giant dragon stretching out:

the mountains form its sturdy bones;

the rivers weave into dense veins;

the forests grow into a lush body and verdant scales;

and civilization forges a soul bold enough to explore and innovate.

![](/images/article/article-1779590707-e60b81/image_10.jpg)

It is the backbone of the Northeast,  
a natural reservoir,  
and the green lung of China.  
With myriad layers inside and out,  
it shapes a landscape both vibrant and diverse!

![](/images/article/article-1779590707-e60b81/image_11.jpg)

**03****Yarlung Tsangpo Grand Canyon — The Last Secret Realm**The world’s highest great river reveals a gentle and serene side where the terrain is flat, with clear waters, slow currents, and a peaceful, tranquil atmosphere~It is a miracle on Earth,  
the pride of the East,  
the last hidden paradise for mankind,  
It lies there,  
waiting for you to explore~

**04****Daocheng Yading——The Last Pure Land on the Blue Planet**Daocheng Yading not only boasts magnificent and sacred snow-capped mountains,

but also endless meadows,

vibrant red beaches,

and crystal-clear turquoise lakes.

Nearly all the most captivating scenery of the snowy plateau is gathered here,

and it makes people fall in love with it without hesitation!The sky is crystal clear, snow-capped mountains gleam white,  
the lake is shimmering and translucent,  
like a fairyland.  
On the road to Yading in Daocheng,  
you’ll never feel lonely~

**05****The Three Gorges of the Yangtze River — "Three Gorges Homeland, a Poetic Three Gorges"**The Three Gorges of the Yangtze River are famous both at home and abroad for their magnificent natural scenery—towering peaks, roaring waves crashing against the cliffs, striking rocks, and misty veils of clouds and fog. They are steeped in beautiful myths and legends, as well as timeless literary works, inspiring endless fascination!The Three Gorges have always been picturesque,

a wonderland unmatched under heaven!

So we can continue the journey that has yet to begin~
