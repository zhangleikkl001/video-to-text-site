Sponsored by "Chinese National Geography," a panel of judges composed of five national professional societies, over a dozen academicians, and nearly a hundred experts and scholars selected winners across 15 categories, including mountains, lakes, forests, grasslands, deserts, Yadan landforms, islands, coastlines, waterfalls, glaciers, canyons, urban areas, ancient villages and towns, tourism caves, and marsh wetlands. Ultimately, several outstanding entries in each category made it to the "Most Beautiful" ranking. Lánhuā Lǚyóu  public account: lanhualvyou

China's six most beautiful lakes:

1. Qinghai Lake (Qinghai). Kanas Lake (Xinjiang). Namtso Lake (Tibet). Heaven Lake on Changbai Mountain (Jilin). Lugu Lake (Yunnan). West Lake in Hangzhou (Zhejiang)

Jin-Shan Grand Canyon (Shanxi and Shaanxi). Jinkou Grand Canyon on the Dadu River (Sichuan)The Six Most Beautiful Caves in China
