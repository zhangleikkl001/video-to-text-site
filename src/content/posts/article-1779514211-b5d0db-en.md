Joint Central-Local Conference on Building Shanghai (Yangtze River Delta) International Science and Technology Innovation Center Held in Shanghai

Jointly Forging a Key Strategic Pillar for Building a Strong Science and Technology Nation

Chen Jining, Yin Hejun, Xin Changxing, Wang Hao, and Liang Yanshun Deliver Speeches; Gong Zheng, Liu Xiaotao, Liu Jie, and Wang Qingxian Attend

Building an international science and technology innovation center is a major strategy personally conceived, deployed, and promoted by General Secretary Xi Jinping. Expanding the Shanghai International Science and Technology Innovation Center to the Yangtze River Delta marks a new phase in the center's development, moving from a single-city breakthrough to regional collaborative and integrated development. This is both a crucial measure to align with the trends of the technological revolution and take the initiative in technological development, and an urgent requirement to deepen coordinated regional development and improve the quality and efficiency of growth.

This afternoon (May 21), the Joint Central-Local Conference on Building Shanghai (Yangtze River Delta) International Science and Technology Innovation Center was held in Shanghai. Shanghai Party Secretary Chen Jining, Minister of Science and Technology Yin Hejun, Jiangsu Party Secretary Xin Changxing, Zhejiang Party Secretary Wang Hao, and Anhui Party Secretary Liang Yanshun attended and delivered speeches. Shanghai Mayor Gong Zheng presided over the meeting. Jiangsu Governor Liu Xiaotao, Zhejiang Governor Liu Jie, and Anhui Governor Wang Qingxian were also present.
