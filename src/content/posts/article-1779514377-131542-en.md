On May 21, the 10th Silk Road International Expo and the China East-West Cooperation and Investment Trade Fair kicked off at the Xi'an International Convention and Exhibition Center.

This year's expo focuses on the theme "New Opportunities for Silk Road Connectivity, New Chapter of Open Cooperation," adopting a "deep integration of online and offline" exhibition model to build a comprehensive platform for economic and trade exchanges,成果展示, and cooperation matching. Six exhibition areas bring together global treasures, inter-provincial cooperation promotes regional linkage, and sectors like aviation low-altitude economy, automotive smart manufacturing, and port-trade-industry integration are showcased. With ten key events and co-host provinces Jiangsu and Yunnan, the expo creates an open future together!

Free and open to all! Six pavilions covering 72,000 square meters—taste food from around the world, explore cutting-edge tech, and score great deals. Whether you're taking the kids, shopping, checking in, or "traveling the globe," it's a one-stop experience. Come unlock the "Silk Road friend circle."

The six pavilions are packed with highlights—no getting lost while exploring.

📍 [Pavilion 1] Silk Road Premium Products・National Pavilion

On-site at the 9th Silk Road Expo 2025  Products from 64 countries gathered, like a "Silk Road world tour." Nearly 200 overseas companies and thousands of specialty items debut, including unique industries, special commodities, and geographical indication products. Free tastings and on-site price comparisons—you can snag authentic global goods right on your doorstep. Perfect for photos and check-ins!

📍 [Pavilion 2] Inter-Provincial Cooperation Pavilion

On-site at the 9th Silk Road Expo 2025  
A national product collection, East-West collaboration full of surprises! Provinces, regions, and Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps showcase industrial achievements, specialty agricultural products, and cultural creative goods. Take in the beauty of the motherland and feel the new vitality of regional coordinated development!

📍 [Pavilion 3] Aviation/Low-Altitude Economy Pavilion

On-site at the 9th Silk Road Expo 2025  Get up close to the technological power of "Shaanxi Smart Manufacturing." Drones, low-altitude aircraft, and hardcore aviation equipment are on display—a must-visit for tech enthusiasts and kids. Enjoy live performances and immersive simulation experiences!

📍 [Pavilion 4] Silk Road Premium Products・Brand Pavilion

On-site at the 9th Silk Road Expo 2025  "Shop in China・Shine in Shaanxi"! Over 150 local Shaanxi brands and more than 1,000 famous specialty products debut, covering food, cultural creations, and tech goods. Bring your family to this "market" and take home novelty and taste.

📍 [Pavilion 5] Automotive Smart Manufacturing Pavilion

On-site at the 7th Silk Road Expo  
Full display of complete vehicles, core components, and smart manufacturing equipment. Get a sneak peek at new energy, smart driving, and future models. Feel the hardcore charm of automotive + smart manufacturing. Come for buying, viewing, or understanding cars.

📍 [Pavilion 6] Port-Trade-Industry Integration Pavilion (China-Europe Railway Economic Circle)  
As a brand-new exhibition area debuting at this expo, it focuses on the Xi'an assembly center of the China-Europe Railway, showcasing the achievements of "channel + trade + industry." Learn how Xi'an connects with the world and experience the tangible benefits brought by the Belt and Road Initiative.
