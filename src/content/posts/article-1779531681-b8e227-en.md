Since the Dongcheng District of Beijing launched the landscape lighting improvement project along the East and North Second Ring Roads at the end of 2025,  
this **8.5-kilometer "light and shadow corridor"** stretching from the North Bridge of the Bell Tower to Dongbianmen Bridge has already shown initial results.  
It is expected to be fully illuminated in the second half of this year.Dongcheng District Children's Palace.Xinda Center.  

Previously, the nightscape along the Second Ring Road from Gulou Bridge in the north to Beijing Station East Street in the south had prominent issues. The lighting on various buildings was scattered, with poor overall coherence, inconsistent styles and brightness, and a lack of coordination.  

To address this, Dongcheng District implemented a nightscape enhancement project covering a total length of 8.5 kilometers, encompassing 41 building groups and 2 scenic nodes. As of now, 31 building groups and 2 scenic nodes have been completed.Hong Kong and Macau Center, Capital Land Tower, Fuhua Building.Zhonghui Plaza.

**PART 08****Daxing Tuanhe Xingguan Night Market**Xingguan Night Market, located in the Tuanhe area of Guanyinsi Street, covers a total planned area of nearly 8,800 square meters.The food court brings together specialties from all over China.  

It offers local traditional flavors like Old Beijing爆肚 (quick-boiled tripe),  

as well as dishes from across the country, such as Jinzhou BBQ and Changsha stinky tofu.  

It also introduces a showcase of Daxing’s local agricultural and creative products,  

including Panggezhuang watermelon cultural merchandise,  

Anding mulberry processed foods,  

and Caiyu grape-derived beverages,  

allowing residents and visitors to taste the regional character of Daxing.

In addition to dining, the night market also features three main sections: a creative crafts area, a daily retail zone, and a family fun zone. These not only meet residents' everyday leisure and shopping needs but also cater to all age groups. The night market is equipped with public restrooms and free drinking water stations, and has staff dedicated to cleaning, crowd management, and food safety inspections to ensure a top-notch experience.Every evening, the central stage of the night market features a series of performances called "Thriving City Never Sleeps." The participating groups include commercial troupes, Daxing District folk art groups, and local school teachers and students. The performances cover a variety of forms such as singing and dancing, traditional opera, cross-talk, clapper talk, and instrumental music. Interactive segments are also included in the shows. In addition to regular performances, the night market will periodically host themed cultural events. During the summer break, there are plans to launch a "Night Reading Market," featuring mobile book stations and a book exchange area, making cultural consumption a new growth point for the night economy.Business hours: Extended appropriately based on the season and customer flow
