**Exhibitions & Brand Events**

**Columbia**

**"The More You Fall, the More You're Hooked" – New Brand Narrative Released**

![](/images/article/article-1779448232-2b0f5c/image_1.jpg)

On April 27, international professional outdoor brand Columbia officially launched its new brand narrative, "The More You Get Into the Wild, the More You Get Hooked," releasing a same-titled ad campaign featuring ambassadors Jiang Qiming and Zhang Jingyi. The campaign focuses on three key outdoor scenarios—hiking, fishing, and trail running—using an anti-perfection, lighthearted humor to capture real, messy, muddy, and unexpected moments that get you "hooked," echoing the brand's slogan "Step into Nature, Nature Plays." Columbia also introduced its Titanium series Wind Sense skin jacket, DRAINMAKER water-cutting shoes, and Kuangfeng XCR third-gen hiking boots, backed by Omni-Shade, Omni-Tech, and Omni-Grip technologies.

Meanwhile, Columbia set up a naked-eye 3D mystery pit installation at its West Bund store in Shanghai, inviting consumers to "jump into the pit" themselves and experience the unique charm of getting more hooked on outdoor adventures the more they go through, with a chance to win gear.

**Longines**

**New HydroConquest Dive Watch**

Longines unveiled its new HydroConquest dive watch at the West Bund Dream Center dock in Shanghai, along with a "Wave-Making Moment" pop-up experience space. The new timepiece comes in a 42mm case, offering four new dials and five ceramic bezel color options, debuting a Milanese mesh bracelet. It is powered by the L888.5 movement with a 72-hour power reserve and water resistance up to 300 meters.

The pop-up space follows a "Wave-Making Journey" theme, featuring eight exhibition zones: an immersive ocean setting with wave visuals and sound from Tenerife, Spain (Wave-Making Moment); a scent memory capturing waves, sea breeze, and shore (Tidal Fragrance); a natural stone bracelet crafting experience (Coast on the Wrist); the new HydroConquest collection on display for try-ons (Timing the New); and a signature beverage to leave a lingering taste (Azure Delight). The space also includes an open-air "Seaside Rest" area and a pet-friendly "Leisure Time" zone. At night, the "Wave-Chasing Theater" transforms into a film and music stage, with afternoon live performances by LEONWILL band, fully embracing the "wave-chasing" theme.

**Feng Ji Sculpture "Rêverie à Giverny"**

**Acquired by the French Impressionist Museum**

Chinese mainland artist FENG J's sculpture "Rêverie à Giverny" has been permanently collected by the French Impressionist Museum (Giverny). The piece captures a dragonfly form in a single cursive stroke, with hand-painted gradient colors and inlaid gemstones like chalcedony and serpentine. It will be displayed alongside 30 original Monet works in the exhibition "Avant les nymphéas" commemorating the 100th anniversary of Monet's death, running until July 5th.
