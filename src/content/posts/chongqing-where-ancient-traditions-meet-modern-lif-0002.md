Welcome to Chongqing, a city that defies all expectations. Here, the 1st floor and the 22nd floor might both lead to the same street. It's a vertical city, stacked on mountains.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif04418/800/600)

At night, Chongqing transforms into a cyberpunk dreamscape. Neon lights reflect off the Yangtze and Jialing rivers, skyscrapers climb impossible slopes.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif14418/800/600)

For photographers, Chongqing is a dream. The layer-cake cityscape, the dramatic light, the river fog that rolls in at dawn.
