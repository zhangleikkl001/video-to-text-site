**Chongqing**

When it comes to nightscapes, Chongqing is, of course, number one.

Surrounded by rivers on three sides, Hongya Cave glitters like a scene straight out of a fairy tale.

Everyone knows that Qingdao’s sea is clean and beautiful, and the city itself is even more so. Actually, Qingdao’s night view is the most stunning!

**Jiang**

At night in Lijiang, the noise of the pedestrian street blends with folk songs drifting from little pubs...

**Beijing**

When talking about Beijing's night, it reminds me of the bustling Wangfujing and Sanlitun, the long queues for crayfish at Gui Street, and the mesmerizing city lights—quiet yet brilliant.**06****Guangzhou**

Guangzhou is a city that never sleeps, with the Canton Tower changing colors and the Zhujiang New Town glittering with lights...

**Chengdu**

In Chengdu, nightlife is a very important part.
