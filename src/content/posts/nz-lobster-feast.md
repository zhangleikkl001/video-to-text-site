Have you ever dreamed of a place where lobsters are so abundant that catching them feels like picking up stones on a beach? 

![Dahao and girlfriend in New Zealand](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1559742811-822873691df8?q=80&w=1000&auto=format&fit=crop)

Popular Douyin creators "Dahao Couple" took us on a wild journey to New Zealand. They heard rumors that the expensive Australian lobsters were "overrunning" the local waters, and they just had to see it for themselves.

### The Great Lobster Hunt
Equipped with diving gear and a lot of excitement, the couple explored the crystal-clear waters of the Kiwi coast. What they found was truly mind-blowing: giant lobsters hidden in every crevice of the underwater rocks.

![Catching giant lobsters](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1625937285773-2c1301c23943?q=80&w=1000&auto=format&fit=crop)

### More Than Just Food
Beyond the thrill of the catch, the video captures the breathtaking scenery of New Zealand and the joyful, adventurous lifestyle of this modern Chinese couple. It's a perfect blend of travel vlog and a "survival" seafood feast.
