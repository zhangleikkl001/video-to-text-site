Twenty-Four Solar Terms

Grain Buds

Grain Buds, the eighth solar term of the twenty-four solar terms and the second of summer, usually arrives when Shanghai is in early summer. It's marked by increased rainfall and stronger precipitation, with more thunderstorm warnings during this period.

Today's Weather

Today, Shanghai will be mostly cloudy to overcast, with occasional light showers in some areas. The northwest wind will pick up in the afternoon. Temperatures are similar to yesterday—morning lows across the city range from 20 to 22°C, and highs range from 26 to 29°C. With lower humidity, higher air pressure, and a northwest wind, it feels much fresher today.

Tomorrow's Weather

Tomorrow, it will be cloudy, turning cloudy to overcast from the afternoon. There may be fog near some areas in the early morning, so be cautious when traveling. Under the influence of an upper trough, it will become overcast with occasional showers at night. East to southeast winds at 4 to 5 levels. Temperatures between 20 and 28°C. Relative humidity 95% to 60%. It will still feel a bit stuffy around midday.

Weather Outlook

In the coming week, with frequent upper trough activity, Shanghai will see multiple rain showers. Rainfall will mainly occur tomorrow night and around next Monday. Temperatures will generally stay between 20 and 28°C. However, humidity remains high, making it feel damp and muggy as usual. Starting next Wednesday, the circulation pattern will shift, bringing back mostly cloudy weather with little temperature change. Please keep an eye on the latest forecasts to plan your activities and travel.
