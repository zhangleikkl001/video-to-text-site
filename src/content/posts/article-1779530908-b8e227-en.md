Since the end of 2025, when Beijing's Dongcheng District launched the landscape lighting improvement project for the East and North Second Ring Roads, this **8.5-kilometer "Light and Shadow Corridor"**, stretching from Zhonglou North Bridge to Dongbianmen Bridge, has already shown initial results. It is expected to be fully illuminated in the second half of this year.Dongcheng District Children’s Palace.Xinda Center.  

Previously, the nightscape along the Second Ring Road from Gulou Bridge in the north to Beijing Station East Street in the south had prominent issues. Building lights were scattered, overall continuity was poor, and styles and brightness levels were inconsistent and uncoordinated.  

To address this, Dongcheng District implemented a nightscape enhancement project spanning 8.5 kilometers, covering 41 building clusters and 2 landscape nodes.  

As of now, 31 building clusters and 2 landscape nodes have been completed.Hong Kong-Macau Center, Capital Land Building, Fuhua Mansion.Zhonghui Plaza.

**PART 08**Daxing Tuanhe Xingguan Night MarketThe Xingguan Night Market, located in the Tuanhe area of Guanyinsi Subdistrict, covers a total planned area of nearly 8,800 square meters.The food court brings together signature snacks from all over the country, featuring local traditional flavors like Old Beijing爆肚, as well as delicacies from across the nation such as Jinzhou barbecue and Changsha stinky tofu. It also introduces Daxing's local specialty agricultural and creative products for exhibition and sale, including Panggezhuang watermelon cultural and creative merchandise, Anding mulberry processed foods, and Caiyu grape derivative drinks, allowing residents and tourists to savor Daxing's regional characteristics through their taste buds.

In addition to dining, the night market also features three functional zones: a cultural and creative handicraft area, a lifestyle retail zone, and a family entertainment section. These not only meet residents' daily leisure and shopping needs but also cater to consumers of all ages. The night market is also equipped with public restrooms, free drinking water stations, and other convenient facilities, with dedicated staff responsible for cleaning, order maintenance, and food safety inspections to ensure a comprehensive consumer experience.Every evening, the central stage of the night market features a series of performances under the "Xingcheng Night Never Ends" program. The participating groups include commercial troupes, Daxing District's folk art groups, and teachers and students from local schools. The performances cover a variety of forms such as singing and dancing, traditional opera, cross-talk, clapper talk, and instrumental music. Interactive segments are also incorporated into the shows. In addition to regular performances, the night market regularly introduces themed cultural events. During the summer, plans include a "Night Reading Market," featuring mobile book points and a book exchange zone, making cultural consumption a new growth point for the night economy.Business hours: Extended appropriately based on the season and visitor flow, with the latest opening until midnight during summer.**PART 09****Yayuncun Beitou Shopping Park Snail Music Festival**In May, the first performance of the "Snail Music Festival," co-hosted by the Asian Games Village Street and Beigou Shopping Park, took place at Snail Square, kicking off the nearly six-month-long music festival.This year's music festival features a complete upgrade of professional lighting and sound, creating an immersive sound wave atmosphere that is fully immersive. The lineup has been expanded with new additions, offering not just classic encores but also exciting new surprises on stage!  

In terms of venue design, the event has added new installations such as a flower fountain and snail-themed lighting decorations, which not only illuminate the core performance area of the "Snail Main Stage" but also create an outdoor music scene that blends artistry with a dreamy feel.  

During the performances, the surrounding area of the square offers themed dining, creative snacks, and trendy pop-up stalls, forming a linked experience of "watching performances + dining + shopping." While enjoying the music, the audience can also grab a bite to eat and browse the stalls.
