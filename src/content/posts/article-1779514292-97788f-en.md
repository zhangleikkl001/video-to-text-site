In the early summer of May, the fragrance of tea is at its peak. The 2026 Beijing International Tea Exhibition, the 2026 Beijing MalianDao International Tea Culture Exchange Conference, and the 2026 Hezhou Tea Promotion Conference will be held simultaneously from May 22 to May 25 at the Beijing Exhibition Center and MalianDao Tea City Square. To help citizens plan their trips and fully enjoy the charm of tea and benefits, we have compiled this detailed guide, inviting you to savor tea and attend this elegant gathering.

01*

2026 Beijing International Tea Exhibition*

Exhibition Visit Reservation:

Free admission, easy to get in

Exhibition Group Highlights:

Two special features, savor diverse tea charms

1. Xicheng Group: Showcasing the distinctive tea culture of MalianDao, creating an immersive experience of "China's First Tea Street." The site brings together diverse tea culture products, including classic jasmine tea from Zhengxingde and Yong'an Tea House, Phoenix Dancong from Tianchi Tea, aged teas from Tianyue and Chenfu White Tea, as well as specialty tea coffee ice cream from Benzhen Coffee, and oriental aesthetics tea ware from Hongjian and Zhuding, catering to citizens' tea tasting and ware appreciation needs.

2. Hezhou Group: As the guest city of honor for this exhibition, Hezhou City from Guangxi presents fine teas such as Liubao tea, Zhaoping black tea, and high-end organic tea. In Hall 2's Hezhou Group area, a special "Taste Hezhou·Freshly Beaten Oil Tea" experience is set up, allowing citizens to not only taste authentic Guangxi tea but also enjoy the unique fragrance of oil tea on site, experiencing the charm of ethnic tea culture.

Consumer Benefits:

Multiple collaborations, saving money and hassle

1. Accommodation Collaboration: From May 21 to May 25, two hotels near the Beijing Exhibition Center offer discounts with a tea exhibition guest badge. The Beijing Exhibition Center Hotel standard room is available at 520 yuan (with single breakfast) or 550 yuan (with double breakfast). With a tea exhibition ticket, enjoy a 15% discount on a la carte dining at the hotel's Chinese and Western restaurants (excluding drinks and seafood, not combinable with other offers). Beijing Guoyi Hotel offers a standard room at 500 yuan (single breakfast) or 540 yuan (double breakfast). In the MalianDao area, Bingtuan Building offers a standard room at 420 yuan (with double breakfast).

2. Market Collaboration: With a physical ticket stub from the tea exhibition or a shopping receipt of 200 yuan or more from the Xicheng Group area, you can receive a 30-yuan-off voucher for purchases over 30 yuan at the "2026 Financial Street·Tea Elegance Carnival" service desk from May 28 to May 31. Limited to 30 vouchers daily, first come, first served.

02*

2026 Beijing MalianDao International Tea Culture*

Exchange Conference and Consumption Promotion Activities

Event Overview:

4-day tea gathering, a date with tea charm

From May 22 to May 25, the 2026 Beijing MalianDao International Tea Culture Exchange Conference will kick off at No. 11 MalianDao Road (in front of MalianDao Tea City), lasting 4 days with continuous tea charm and highlights.

Exhibition Fun:

Great products gathered, performances non-stop

This exchange conference brings together high-quality tea-producing regions and renowned tea enterprises nationwide, integrating intangible cultural heritage, special cultural tourism products, and rich performances. The market features over 40 booths, covering three categories: tea, specialty products from tea-producing areas, and food. Key displays include Hezhou hawthorn bud black tea, taro, high-quality rice, vegetables for Hong Kong, Xinhui tangerine peel and citrus pu'er tea, peanut derivatives, and Guizhou tea, along with special snacks like intangible cultural heritage freshly beaten glutinous rice cakes.

On-site, you can taste various teas and learn about tea culture. On the evenings of May 22 to 24, there will be a rotation of intangible cultural heritage solar term talks, folk arts, and cross talk performances. Whether you're a seasoned tea enthusiast for tea talks, or a citizen on a leisure outing or family stroll, you can enjoy a cozy and satisfying tea time.

Exclusive Perks:

Check-in interactions, gifts galore

1. Gift with Purchase: Spend at any shop within MalianDao Tea City to redeem a limited edition custom canvas bag.

2. Interactive Coupons: Partnering with Tiansheng Group for "My Red Mansion World" NPC game interactions. Participating consumers can play emoji guessing and arrow-pitch games, complete quiz tasks, and receive limited 50-yuan vouchers for the "Rugao Moga" Dunhuang exhibition at Beijing Exhibition Center and dining coupons from nearby shops.

3. Stamp Rally: Six designated stamp points are set up in MalianDao Tea City. Collect 3 stamps to redeem a limited tea bag blind box, or collect 6 stamps for a limited tea gift box. The first 50 citizens to collect all stamps daily receive an extra tea coffee drink or a little lion ice cream. All gifts are limited daily, first come, first served.
