The food here is legendary. Nothing represents the city's spirit better than a bubbling pot of hotpot — a communal dining experience where diners cook ingredients in a cauldron of spicy broth, the steam mingling with laughter and conversation.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif02098/800/600)

Welcome to Chongqing, a city that defies all expectations. Here, the 1st floor and the 22nd floor might both lead to the same street. It's a vertical city, stacked on mountains.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif12098/800/600)

For photographers, Chongqing is a dream. The layer-cake cityscape, the dramatic light, the river fog that rolls in at dawn — every angle tells a different story of modern China.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif22098/800/600)

Getting around is an adventure in itself. The subway system is a marvel of engineering, diving through mountains and crossing rivers. And then there are the escalators — some stretching nearly 20 stories long.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif32098/800/600)

Unlike Beijing or Shanghai, Chongqing has a raw, unfiltered energy. It's one of China's fastest-growing cities, yet it retains a character that feels authentic and unpolished. Every corner reveals something unexpected.

![](https://picsum.photos/seed/chongqing-where-ancient-traditions-meet-modern-lif42098/800/600)
