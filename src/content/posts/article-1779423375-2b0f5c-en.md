**Exhibitions & Brand Events**

**Columbia**

**"The More You Fall, the More You're Hooked" New Brand Story Released**

On April 27, international outdoor brand Columbia officially launched its new brand narrative, "The More You Fall, the More You're Hooked," alongside brand ambassadors Jiang Qiming and Zhang Jingyi in a new ad film. Focusing on hiking, fishing, and trail running, the campaign uses a lighthearted, anti-perfection style to showcase real outdoor moments of mud, mishaps, and surprises, echoing the brand slogan "Step into nature, and naturally play." The brand also released tech gear like the Titanium Series Windbreaker, DRAINMAKER water-cutting shoes, and Kuanfeng Series XCR Gen 3 hiking boots, featuring Omni sun protection, waterproofing, and grip technologies.

Meanwhile, Columbia set up a naked-eye 3D "mystery pit" installation at its West Bund store in Shanghai, inviting consumers to "fall into the pit" and experience the unique charm of outdoor adventures that only get more addictive with each challenge. Lucky visitors could also win gear.

**Longines**

**New HydroConquest Diving Watch Collection**

Longines unveiled its new HydroConquest diving watch collection and the "Wave-Making Moment" pop-up experience at the West Bund Dream Center shipyard dock in Shanghai. The new model comes in a 42mm size with four new dials and five ceramic bezel color options, featuring a Milanese mesh bracelet for the first time. Powered by the L888.5 movement, it boasts a 72-hour power reserve and 300-meter water resistance.

The pop-up space follows a "Wave-Making Journey" theme, divided into eight zones: an immersive ocean soundscape with footage from Tenerife, Spain (Wave-Making Moment), a scent experience capturing sea breeze and sand (Tidal Fragrance), a DIY natural stone bracelet station (Coast at Your Wrist), a try-on area for the new HydroConquest watches (Time Control Zone), and signature drinks (Azure Bliss). There's also an open-air "Seaside Retreat" and a pet-friendly "Leisure Time" area; at night, the "Wave Chaser Theater" transforms into a stage for film and music, with live performances by the LEONWILL band in the afternoon, all embracing the "surfing the waves" theme.

**Feng Ji's Sculpture "Rêverie à Giverny"**

**Acquired by the French Impressionist Museum**

Chinese mainland artist Feng Ji's (FENG J) sculpture "Rêverie à Giverny 浮生梦" was permanently collected by the Musée des Impressionnismes Giverny. The piece features a dragonfly sketched in one cursive stroke, with hand-painted gradient colors and inlaid gemstones like chalcedony and serpentine. It will be exhibited alongside 30 original Monet works at the grand exhibition "Avant les nymphéas" commemorating the 100th anniversary of Monet's death, running until July 5.
