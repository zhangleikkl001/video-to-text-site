Wind dyes early summer, wheat waves turn golden

As the sun gently spreads over the Zhongnan Mountains

Over the Guanzhong Plain

A touching scene of an agricultural civilization spanning millennia

Unfolds once again

Recently, the highly anticipated

8th Guanzhong Mangba Art Festival

Officially kicked off in Caijiapo Village, Huyi District, Xi'an

Pictures by Huyi Publicity and Guanzhong Art Cooperative

This year's art festival

Centers on "Enriching the Countryside with Art and Literature"

Takes Huyi District as its main stage

Extending to the northern foothills of the Qinling Mountains, including Chang'an, Zhouzhi, and Lantian

Aiming to build a model art village belt

It features six major themes and seven key activities

Running throughout the year, taking place at multiple points

Spanning from May to October

With the goal of bringing art

Truly into the fields and farmlands

And integrating it into everyday life
