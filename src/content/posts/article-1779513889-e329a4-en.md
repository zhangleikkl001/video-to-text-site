The "Veteran" of the Baijiu World self-built a 300-acre seaside utopia!

Today's recommendation, the Sanya Haitang Bay Moutai Resort, can be called a "tipsy paradise" for alcohol lovers. As the name suggests, the entire resort is self-built and operated by the "national liquor" Guizhou Moutai, featuring four hotels named after Moutai's sauce-aroma series: Zunpin, Zhenpin, Jingpin, and Dianpin.

After landing at Sanya Phoenix Airport, a 40-minute drive will take you to the resort. From Yalong Bay Station, it's only a 20-minute drive.

Image | Ctrip Community

The resort is about a 20-minute drive from two top snorkeling spots: Houhai and Wuzhizhou Island. Wuzhizhou Island has clear waters suitable for beginners and offers romantic coral reef scenery. Houhai has clearer water and fewer people, making it ideal for casual snorkeling and free diving enthusiasts.

This time, we mainly introduce the "classic" Sanya Moutai Zhenpin Hotel, which also houses the most guest rooms among the four hotels, serving as the "main force."

Image | Ctrip Community

"Moutai elements" are everywhere inside the hotel—a Flying Moutai wall made of 1,680 Moutai bottles, a map of the South China Sea pieced together from bottle caps... adding a unique sauce-aroma mark to the entire trip.

You can also taste Moutai ice cream, Moutai flame shrimp, and other delicacies, letting the rich liquor aroma blend with island flavors, creating a new taste experience.

Image | Ctrip Community 
A Luxurious Experience Under the Blue Sky and Sea

The hotel is located in the center of Haitang Bay, so the rooms offer a comfortable experience "near the sea but without the tide." Head to the beach directly via its own underground passage connecting to the sea, and you'll encounter the romantic summer seaside vibe in just three minutes. You can also take the resort's shuttle bus to enjoy the scenery along the way and easily reach the beach.

At the same time, the resort's shuttle bus also connects to Sanya International Duty-Free City, so staying at this hotel gives you discounts on some items.

Image | Ctrip Community 
Although the resort enjoys a romantic seaside atmosphere, it also features a variety of water activities. A long immersive circular drift river, a refreshing open-air pool, thrilling skateboarding surfing, and a large water park covering nearly 4,500 square meters—you can enjoy summer fun without even stepping out.

Image | Ctrip Community

In addition, the resort offers exciting experiences like aerial ropeways, go-kart clubs, and indoor golf, waiting to be unlocked, so your holiday won't be boring.

Image | Ctrip Community

The regular price for the basic room, the Zhenpin Luxury King Room, starts at around 700+ per night. The overall design isn't overly cluttered, and the simple, elegant light gray gives a sense of openness. The rooms also come with a private balcony, offering views of distant mountains and the sea merging with the sky, letting you fully enjoy the freedom and comfort of the holiday.

Now, during the 618 event, it's reduced to 799 for two nights. Good deals are worth stocking up on, so grab one during the event!
