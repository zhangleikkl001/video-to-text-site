**Nanluoguxiang**

Beijing

![](/images/article/article-1779593984-7d2555/image_4.jpg)

Nanluoguxiang

It was built during the same period as the Yuan Dynasty's capital

and is one of the oldest neighborhoods in Beijing

with a history of over 740 years

Because the terrain was higher in the middle and lower in the north and south

like a hunched back man

it was named Luoguo (Hunchback) Lane

It wasn't until the Qing Dynasty that it was renamed Nanluoguxiang

**Hefang Street**

AnhuiHefang Street lies at the foot of Wu Hill,  
a story that can't be left untold about old Hangzhou.  
It was once the "foot of the imperial city" in ancient capital Hangzhou,  
having witnessed the prosperity of the Southern Song Dynasty,  
as well as the daily bustle of the Ming and Qing dynasties.  
Hangzhou's famed "Five Hangzhou Products"—  
Hangzhou face powder, Hangzhou scissors, Hangzhou fans, Hangzhou tobacco, and Hangzhou thread—all originated here.by Tuchong F_Y_UTranslator: Tuchong Townlight
