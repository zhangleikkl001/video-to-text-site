As one of Xi’an’s most iconic eco-cultural destinations, Bailu Plain perfectly blends the sweet aroma of fresh fruits, Guanzhong folk customs, tranquil bamboo forests, and a rich humanistic film and television heritage. It offers a one-stop experience combining fruit picking, leisure, sightseeing, and study tours. Save this guide to Bailu Plain, and when the weather clears up, gather your family and friends to embrace the early summer on the plateau!

Bailuyuan Cherry Valley is one of the core production areas of the national geographical indication product "Baqiao Cherry," cultivating multiple high-quality cherry varieties. In early summer in May, the valley is adorned with red pearls nestled among the green leaves, within easy reach. Carrying fruit baskets, wander through the woods, feel the healing scent of fruit lingering around. Pick cherries and experience the joy of the harvest.

The scenic area blends the market scenes of the late Qing and early Republic eras  
with authentic folk customs and trendy experiences,  
so that every step reveals the rustic charm of centuries-old Shaanxi.

Republic-era Street is a must-see highlight,  
faithfully recreating the look of streets and alleys from that period.  
Landmarks like the Paramount Ballroom and the Wang Residence  
largely restore the era’s architectural style.

Nearby, the ancient street showcases the traditional flavor of Guanzhong.  
Rammed-earth courtyard walls, gray-tiled gate towers,  
and folk craft workshops dot the area,  
vividly presenting the life of plateau dwellers in the late Qing and early Republic.

Image/ Baqiao Culture and Tourism

Image: Qujiang Events, Xi'an Whale Gully Bamboo Sea Scenic Area  

At the Whale Gully Bamboo Sea Scenic Area  

Escape the hustle and bustle, enjoy the tranquility of early summer  

The scenic area leans against the Qinling Mountains to the south and overlooks the Wei River to the north  

It combines the elegant beauty of waterfront scenery  

With the majestic views of the highlands  

This is the largest  

original ecological bamboo sea growing area in Northwest China  

Known as the "Guanzhong Bamboo Sea"  

Endless stretches of green bamboo block out the sun, staying lush all year roundThe scenic area has an extremely high concentration of negative oxygen ions.  
The air is fresh, refreshing and invigorating.  

Strolling through, it cleanses your lungs and calms your mind.  

At the end of the trail, Whale Lake glistens with rippling blue waves,  
surrounded by green mountains on both sides, with bamboo reflections shimmering in the water.  

You can take a bamboo raft or speedboat to glide across the lake, enjoying the scenery of mountains and waters.  

The sound of flowing water is soothing, cool, and delightful.

Image/Qujiang Events, Xi'an Whale Gully Bamboo Sea Scenic Area

Photo: Shaanxi Tourism White Deer Plain Film and TV City

White Deer Plain Film and TV City

With the core of "restoring the Guanzhong landscape and passing on the hometown cultural heritage,"

It features diverse areas such as White Deer Village, Zishui County Town,

scenic walkways, and a creative arts district.

This is the real-life recreation of the novel *White Deer Plain*,

with highly detailed replicas of Bai Jiaxuan's home, Lu Zilin's home,

and Tian Xiao'e's cave dwelling.

Every brick and tile carries the rural memories of the plateau.

Photo: Shaanxi Tourism White Deer Plain Film and Television City  

Cultural performances are the core highlight of the scenic area.  

The large-scale live-action stunt show *Two Tigers Guarding Chang'an* recreates the heroic history of Yang Hucheng and Li Hucheng leading the soldiers and civilians to defend Chang'an.  

*The Legend of Heiwa* integrates filming, performance, and screening into one, allowing visitors to become "extra actors" and immerse themselves in the life experience of "Heiwa."
