**Columbia**

**"Nature Gets Better the Deeper You Fall" New Brand Story Unveiled**

On April 27, international outdoor brand Columbia officially launched its new brand narrative "Nature Gets Better the Deeper You Fall," releasing a campaign film with brand ambassadors Jiang Qiming and Zhang Jingyi. **Feng Ji's Sculpture "Floating Life Dream"**

**Permanently Collected by the French Impressionist Museum**

Chinese mainland artist Feng J's sculpture "Rêverie à Giverny" (Floating Life Dream) has been permanently collected by the French Impressionist Museum (Giverny). The piece outlines a dragonfly in one cursive stroke, with hand-painted blended colors on the surface, inlaid with gems like chalcedony and serpentine jade. It will be exhibited alongside 30 original Monet works in the major exhibition "Avant les nymphéas" commemorating the 100th anniversary of Monet's death, running until July 5.
